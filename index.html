Privacy Policy for DeDupe2Activate

This extension accesses your browser tab information (URLs and navigation events) solely to detect and remove duplicate tabs.

Data Collection:

  • We access tab URLs to identify duplicates
  • We monitor tab creation and navigation events
  • No personal information is collected
  • No data is stored permanently
  • No data is shared with third parties

Data Usage

All data is processed locally in your browser and used only for duplicate tab detection and removal.
