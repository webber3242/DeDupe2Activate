Privacy Policy - DeDupe2Activate

This extension automatically closes duplicate tabs.

What it needs access to

Your tab URLs and tab activity - so it can tell which tabs are duplicates.

What it does

Finds tabs with the same URL, keeps the newer one, closes the old one. Everything happens in your browser.

What it doesn't do

Doesn't save anything. Doesn't send data anywhere. Just looks at your tabs temporarily to do the duplicate checking.

Click "Commit changes" Go to Settings → Pages → Source: "Deploy from a branch" → Branch: "main" → Save Your URL will be: https://yourusername.github.io/dedupe-privacy
